Sweet Honey

Made and processed for direct consumption. This product has benefits of antioxidants, helping with cough and sore throat, and immunity boost. Our honeycomb is high in minerals, Vitamin B and C. It has a soft and golden texture that comes with a mild sweet and sour savor. We would recommend a daily consumption of 250 grams to help alleviate sneezing, runny nose and watery eyes especially during allergy seasons for those individuals out there (disclaimer: this is just a general representation, it might not work for you and we will not be liable if it doesn't work out for you).

Informations

Storage for All Products :) Please do not place in refrigerators Prevent exposing to direct sunlight (Best kept under shades where humidity is low) There is no expiry for raw honey Way of Consuming our Honey Products Direct consumption: 1 tablespoon(15g) scooped directly and consumed Beverage : Recommended to use 2 tablespoon(30g) of honey in a cup(250ml) of water Infants under the age of 12 months are not advisable to consume
